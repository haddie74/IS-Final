WHICH STREAMING SERVICE?

Spotify. My family pays for the family plan so I never even looked at apple music. And at this point I am hundreds of playlists too deep to even think about switching. I also love how much effort Spotify puts into crafting my daily mixes and other personalized playlists. Because of that, I am able to find new music and new artists daily! If you currently use Apple music, you should really make the switch to Spotify.

MONTHLY PLAYLISTS

Back in 2019, I was really getting into finding and listening to new songs constantly. I had my regular playlists, but I needed a place to organize all my current favorites. The idea came to have a new playlist every month! I decided to try it out for a few months, and I fell in love with it. I now had a place to organize the songs I was obessing over, and had a soundtrack for each month saved. To this day, I make a playlist monthly, and it has become a journal of sorts. For example, March and April of 2020 have particularly memory-inducing playlists because I was listening to music even more during the pandemic. Here's my resume if you want your own playlist!
